Understanding Turkish Standards: Ensuring Quality and Safety

Navigating Turkish Standards: Upholding Quality and Safety

Turkish standards refer to the national standards and technical regulations that are established by the Turkish Standards Institution (Türk Standardları Enstitüsü or TSE) in Turkey. These standards are used to ensure the quality, safety, and compatibility of products, processes, and services in various industries. The TSE is the national standardization body responsible for developing, maintaining, and promoting these standards in Turkey.
